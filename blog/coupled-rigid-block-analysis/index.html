Coupled Rigid-Block Analysis (CRA) — Published in CAD

Our latest research on Coupled Rigid-Block Analysis (CRA) is published in the SCI journal Computer-Aided Design.
February 8, 2022 · 1 min · Gene Ting-Chun Kao

I am thrilled to share our latest exciting research — Coupled Rigid-Block Analysis (CRA) — published in the SCI journal Computer-Aided Design.

CRA is a new method to measure structural stability statically and can be used to design complex discrete-element assemblies. It can be applied to many research fields like designing architectures, furniture, 3D puzzles, toys, or even robotic assembly planning. Please see our open-access paper for more details!

→ Read the open-access paper

Also, thanks to everyone who helped with this research, including but not limited to Keerthana, Francesco, Sam, Alessandro, Noelle, and my good friends Lenny and Lucas!
